sample URL 1:

/test/folder/image.jpg?x=1&y=2#foo

splitting at #:

save "/test/folder/image.jpg?x=1&y=2" in wihtout_fragment, save "foo" in fragment

splitting at ?:

save "/test/folder/image.jpg" in full_path, save "x=1&=2" in raw_parameters



types of URLs:

"/test/folder/image.jpg" -> no fragment + query

"test/folder/image.jpg#foo" -> no query

"test/folder/image.jpg?x=1&y=2#foo" -> with both
